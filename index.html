How to play

GOAL! GOAL! GOAL! uses simple tap-based controls that are easy to learn and quick to master.

Move & position
Tap anywhere on the field and the closest player will move to that position.
Pass & shoot
Tap a teammate to pass the ball, or tap when in possession to shoot on goal.
Defend & intercept
Position your players in passing lanes to intercept the ball and switch quickly to attack.
Attack & defend
Every player participates in both attack and defense, keeping the game fast and dynamic.
